Lasagna

Description

This exellent lasagna recipe will take less than two hours between preparation and cooking.

It has a few steps, but they are easy to follow even for a novice chef.

Summary: Prep Time 30 minutes, Cook Time 1 hour, Resting Time 15 minutes.

Ingredients for 12 servings

  • Spices
  • Ricotta or cottage cheese
  • Mozarella
  • Parmesan
  • A good tomato sauce - probably, the most important ingredient
  • Tomato paste
  • Parsley
  • Egg
  • Lasagna
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Beef & sausage meet

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water
  2. Prepare the meat sauce. Coof beef and sausage with onion and garlic, add the pasta sauce and simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture and stir it together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer 4x the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.

Tips

Each layer is made of 1. sauce; 2. noodles, 3. cheese mix. except for the last, which is made by noodles, sauce (bake) and cheese.

Bake process: Cover with foil and bake. Remove foil, top with mozzarella and parmesan, and bake for another 15 minutes.
